Why should I support OPTC-info?

As fan-project, OPTC-info is made in my free time and I simply enjoy doing it.

However, it's very time-consuming to make nice-looking crafts and in order to deliver them before Christmas, I skipped my OPTC play-time, weekends and even sleep last few weeks :D
I polished them good enough for use, but there is room for improvements.

With some community support, I could turn down a bit of extra work and redeem myself time to make things we all enjoy instead :)

You have no obligation to help the project in any way. All content is free for personal use. It's entirely up to you. But I'll be very thankful and keep doing nice stuff.
